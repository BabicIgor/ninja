our mission

Net Ninjas’ platform will help reduce the global electricity consumtion
by supporting decentralized infrastructure. We aim to provide safe,
transparent, and accessible tools for renting your personal device
(phone, CPU, hard drive, GPU…) to our global network of partners.
A portion of the profits will be used to support subsidized
infrastructure for social impact and human rights projects.
